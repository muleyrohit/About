Short Introduction:


I come from an engineering background, which is ironic, for my mental preferences align more towards philosophy and the fine arts. That makes me a chimera of sorts, without a specific niche.

I love reading and thinking. I believe in doing things slowly and deliberately, and not ‘figuring things out as I go’.

I am a minimalist, and I prefer fine things. I also enjoy anything that has a rhythm to it, from cooking and music, to even cleaning and tidying up stuff.
I dislike sloppy thinking and regression to defaults.

I live by the mental-models of the Anti-Library, Non-Rigorous Scientific Intuition, and Asymmetry.

I am a great conversationalist and reasonably pleasant company, well, unless you catch me in one of my not infrequent moods.
But those can easily be taken care of with a nice cup of Coffee :).
